Stuck in Time

Virtual World, Blender/Moilla Hubs, 2021

This project was a representation of the feelings that I, and many others worldwide, have felt through the COVID19 pandemic. For a while, time seemed to have stopped due to the pandemic, especially during the first year. The hourglass in the world represents the feeling that time has stopped or moving very slowly. In addition to time moving slowly, we have also had many overwhelming issues that arised such as wearing masks, getting the vaccine, and the virus itself. These overwhelming issues are represesnted in the world as well through the different 3D models implemented into the world.
